Date: Wednesday, 02 March 2022 14:52:23 UTC
From: Dave (K8RDG/4)
Thank you for the well-written information. I, too, have the Super Antennas MP-1 and the MFJ-1788. Your information and experience with these two antennas was helpful and confirmed many of my findings for both. When I was looking for a portable antenna to use from the balcony of my FL QTH, I started with the MP-1 with mediocre results. Next I tried the MFJ-1788 and achieved improved results. I finally adjusted the internal loop of the MFJ-1788 and improved the SWR as well as achieving increased QSOs. Although tuning is a little tricky, it's convenient and easily mastered. And although QSOs are difficult, and propagation is dynamic, the MFJ-1788 performs well in the horizontal position, considering my restrictions. Thanks again for your info.
